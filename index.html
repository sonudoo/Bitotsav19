    About Us

    From the ‘Khokhloma’ painting symbolizing the high importance of forests and nature in Russian culture and its history; to ‘Liwa’ the traditional dance performed in Eastern Arabia; from the traditional Chinese ‘Qipao’ dress, to over 720 dialects and 22 official languages in India; form ‘yakuniku’ a Japanese style of cooking, to ‘Teochew opera’ in Singapore; a deep vein of history courses through all the countries of Asia. Through the theme “Celebration of Cultures-Colours of Asia” BITOTSAV’19 procures to contribute in its own distinctive way to celebrate the rich, large, complex and variegated cultures of humanity.

    Lost in Echoes (Social Campaign)

    If we trace the trails of time, we come across various art forms, be it music, dance or the fine arts, which have, over time, lost their sheen and significance. From the Madhubani painting of Bihar to the Toda embroidery of the Nilgiri hills shows us our glorious past are gradually attriting and it is about time that we do something about it. In an effort to revive the magnificence of our rich culture, we at BITOTSAV'19 have pledged to bring to the fore, such art forms via various awareness campaigns, workshops and talks.

    विशेष (Social Cause)

    दिव्यांग्ता प्राय: जन्म से, परिस्थितिजन्य अथवा दुर्घटना के कारण होती है। हमारे देश मे आज भी लगभग 3 करोड़ लोग दिव्यांग है जिनमे 50% अशिक्षित है। सर्वविदित है कि समाज मे दिव्यांगजन की स्थिति चुनौतीपूर्ण होती है।आज के युग में भी दिव्यांग को दया, करुणा और सहानुभूति का पात्र समझा जाता है। इस बितोत्सव, हम न केवल इस बदलाव की सराहना करते है बल्कि साथ ही यह संकल्प लेते है कि विकलांगता से दिव्यांगता के इस सफर मे हुम भी भागीदार होंगे और दिव्यांगो के प्रति लोगों की सहानुभूति को सहयोग मे परिवर्तित करने मे बढ चढ़कर हिस्सा लेंगे।

    Responsible Netizenship (Online Campaign)

    The internet is not restricted to a set of rules and regulations and is subject to use as and how desired by the end consumer. A responsible netizen, is a person who has a moral approach towards the usage of the internet: one who respects the online community, and abides by the so-called 'Netiquettes'. To spread awareness regarding the same, this year's social campaign for BITOTSAV is chosen as Responsible Netizenship. The entire BITOTSAV'19 team will strive to ensure that people learn about the duties and responsibilities of a netizen.
