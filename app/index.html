Bitotsav '19 App



With the advent of wireless communication and the world wide web, the transfer of information has become easier than ever. In a dynamic event like Bitotsav where around 45 events will be conducted in just three days, efficient propagation of information is really important. But you do not have to worry as we have you covered. Ladies and gentlemen, the Bitotsav '19 App is live. From venue changes to points table, stay up to date on everything that happens during the extravaganza.



Features


  • Event Registration: Register your team for flagships and formal/ informal events
  • Personalised Feed: Get updates related to subscribed events, team wins etc.
  • Profile: Login with Bitotsav Id to access Team Statistics and Achievements
  • Individual Registration: Register and get your Bitotsav Id.
  • Day-Wise Events: Full events listed sorted by time, with venue & time.
  • Event Details: Select any event to get detailed information, contact coordinators, see results etc.
  • Subscription: Subscribe to events you like to receive personalised alerts and updates.
  • Global Feed: Real time updates from the Bitotsav '19 team.
